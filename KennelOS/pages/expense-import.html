Import Expenses (CSV)

Bring receipts and mileage/trip logs into the Financials ledger — the export a companion receipts app produces. A row with miles set is a mileage expense (its amount is figured from miles × rate); otherwise it's a flat expense. The preview lets you attach each expense to a dog, litter, pairing, or your kennel before saving (prefilled from the file). Re-importing the same file updates rather than duplicates.
